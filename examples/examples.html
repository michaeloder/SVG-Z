A 4×4 control point grid defines a bicubic Bézier patch. This saddle is the classic hyperbolic paraboloid — y = x² − z².

Three semi-transparent planes intersect at a white sphere. WBOIT (Weighted Blended OIT) ensures correct blending from any view angle.

The cross-section direction rotates 90° from left to right across the patch. The control points encode the twist using parametric rotation.

A 270° rotation exposes the interior. cap-start and cap-end seal the cut faces with flat polygons.
